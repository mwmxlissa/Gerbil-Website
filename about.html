Care Tips üêæ

To keep your gerbil happy and healthy, provide them with a spacious habitat. A glass tank or wire cage with a solid floor is ideal. Be sure to add plenty of bedding for digging and nesting. A balanced diet is key to their health. Feed them pelleted food, seeds, fresh vegetables, and the occasional fruit. Always make sure they have access to fresh water.Gerbils need plenty of exercise to stay fit and entertained. Toys, tunnels, and a hamster wheel will help keep them physically and mentally active.

Why We Love Gerbils

Gerbils are social, curious, and low-maintenance pets that bring joy to families of all ages. Their playful nature and minimal care needs make them ideal for both new and experienced pet owners.
